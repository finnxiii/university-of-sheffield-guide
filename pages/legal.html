Data Privacy and Legal Compliance

Our website complies with copyright and GDPR by using only licensed images and collecting minimal data. The contact form requests only a user’s name and email to keep the information collection limited and privacy-friendly. Since the form does not send emails, data is retained solely within our system for feedback review, minimizing external data transmission risks (Intersoft Consulting, 2018).

Security and Accessibility Measures

For security, we ensure that data collected is encrypted during transmission through HTTPS, protecting against third-party interception (OWASP, 2024). Additionally, semantic HTML5 elements like <form> and <footer> support organized data handling and promote accessibility.
